## Research Incorporated

### Major Studies Referenced:

1. **MIT Researchers (2025)**
   - 95% of generative AI pilots failing
   - Organizations that redesign workflows first = 2x ROI
   - Learning gap: people don't understand how to USE AI

2. **S&P Global Market Intelligence (1,000+ enterprises)**
   - 42% abandoned AI projects in 2025 (up from 17% in 2024)
   - Average org scrapped 46% of AI POCs before production

3. **Microsoft/IDC Global Study**
   - $3.50 return per $1 invested (average)
   - Top 5% achieve $8 per $1 invested
   - Bottom performers: $0.59 per $1 (losing money)

4. **RAND Corporation Analysis**
   - 80%+ AI project failure rate
   - 2x higher than non-AI technology projects

5. **Forrester Total Economic Impact Study**
   - 330% ROI over 3 years
   - 210% average ROI
   - Payback in under 6 months (for companies following best practices)

6. **Stanford Digital Economy Lab (SMB Study)**
   - 72% of SMBs optimistic about AI
   - 62% lack in-house expertise
   - Only 14% of small businesses use AI vs 34% of large companies

7. **Failure Factor Research**
   - 71.7% cite knowledge gaps
   - 70% hit technical challenges
   - 67% lack proper training
   - 51% insufficient knowledge at management level

8. **McKinsey/IBM Studies**
   - 56% adopted AI in at least one function
   - Average enterprise ROI: 5.9% (below cost of capital for many)
   - 40% looking to invest due to generative AI potential
