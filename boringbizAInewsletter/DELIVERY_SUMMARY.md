## What It Does

Creates 3 artifacts from your source material:

### Artifact 1: Main Newsletter
- 1000-2000 words
- Opening hook (150-300 words)
- Today's overview (3 bullet points)
- Main content (800-1500 words)
- Ready to publish

### Artifact 2: Liz's Corner
- 500-1000 words
- Practical, actionable guide
- Tool comparisons or step-by-step process
- Includes pricing, timelines, pros/cons
- Complements main newsletter

### Artifact 3: Marketing Elements
- 10 subject line options (varied styles)
- 9 image/visual prompts (detailed descriptions)
- Organized by purpose (main/Liz's/general)

## Key Features

✓ **Voice consistency** - Always "we/our", never "I"

✓ **Action-focused** - Every piece immediately actionable
